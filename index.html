About

Where precision meets purpose.

I have spent over 15 years inside large, complex organisations — the kind where data is everywhere but insight is rare. At Ikeja Electric I built analytics and AI systems that actually changed how decisions were made at the executive level. Not dashboards that nobody looks at. Real frameworks that leaders trusted and acted on.

My work sits at the intersection of AI deployment, analytics governance, and leadership decision-making. I have done this across energy, banking, and telecoms — sectors where the stakes are high and the margin for error is thin. I know what it takes to move AI from a PowerPoint slide into daily operations.

Outside the boardroom I write books, produce films, and speak at conferences around the world. Not because it is part of a personal brand strategy — but because I genuinely believe that how you tell a story matters as much as the data behind it. The two things feed each other in ways I never expected.
